Animator Controls

How to Use:

  1. Upload a field image/PDF & initial robot sprite.
  2. Optional: Disable background to record with solid color.
  3. Click on the field to add waypoints (even just 1!).
  4. Drag waypoints to reposition them.
  5. Select a waypoint to edit heading and actions.
  6. Use "Add Sprite Action" or "Duplicate" for stop-motion.
  7. Adjust rotation for each action to rotate sprites.
  8. Press 'Animate' to preview or 'Record' to save.
  9. While recording: Press SPACE to pause, then drag to pan or scroll to zoom!
